About School

The Mehran School is situated in the Jamshoro Sindh. This institution was established in April 2007 for the betterment of Rural Area. In this institution there are almost 1000+ students are studying. This is an organised and well established institution in which Play group to intermediate classes are being taken. There are physics, chemistry and Biology Labs in this institution and also a well developed and updated Computer lab. In this institution there is Computer and Biology Science facilities for Girls.
